# Core Gameplay Loop

## The Loop (Simple Steps)
This is the foundational cycle that drives the entire game.

### 1. **Select Race + Job**
- Player chooses from 5 races × 5 jobs (25 combinations)
- Race determines HP, speed, inventory size
- Job determines weapon/armor access and abilities

### 2. **Enter Dungeon**
- Arrive at dungeon entrance (hub area)
- Can visit shop to buy/sell items
- Configure initial inventory loadout
- Enter dungeon

### 3. **Explore Dungeon Section**
- Navigate rooms, corridors, hazards
- Fight enemies using real-time combat
- Find loot (weapons, armor, consumables, gold)
- Make inventory decisions (pickup, drop, swap)
- Solve simple environmental puzzles

### 4. **Reach Checkpoint**
- Mid-section rest room
- Can swap inventory with vault storage
- Heal to full HP/stamina
- Prepare for next section or mid-boss

### 5. **Fight Mid-Boss** (optional)
- Test of current loadout
- Rewards: Gold, unique materials, key items
- Checkpoint after victory

### 6. **Repeat Steps 3-5** for sections 2-3

### 7. **Fight Dungeon Boss**
- Final challenge requiring right tools
- Tests inventory choices, job abilities, positioning
- Victory grants: Gold, dungeon material, story progress

### 8. **Return to Hub**
- Spend gold on weapon upgrades
- Unlock new dungeon paths
- Take on side quests
- Restock consumables

### 9. **Repeat for 8 Dungeons**
- Each dungeon ~1 hour
- Progressive difficulty
- Unique themes and mechanics

### 10. **Face Final Boss**
- The Vault Sovereign
- Tests ALL systems: inventory, job abilities, upgrades, preparation
- Multiple endings based on choices

---

## Minute-to-Minute Gameplay

**Combat (30-40% of time):**
- Move with WASD
- Attack with mouse clicks
- Dodge with spacebar
- Block with shift (if shield equipped)
- Use consumables with 1-4 hotkeys

**Exploration (30-40% of time):**
- Navigate rooms
- Avoid/trigger traps
- Find chests and secrets
- Read environmental storytelling

**Inventory Management (20-30% of time):**
- Decide what to pick up
- Swap gear mid-dungeon
- Drop items to make room
- Use consumables strategically

---

## Core Tension
**"Do I pick this up or leave room for something better?"**

This question drives every inventory decision and creates replayability.

---

## Win Condition
- Complete 8 dungeons
- Defeat The Vault Sovereign
- Your ending depends on:
  - Race/job combination
  - Inventory at final boss
  - Gold spent vs. saved
  - Side quests completed

---

## Lose Condition
- Die in dungeon → respawn at last checkpoint, lose progress
- Optional permadeath mode (hardcore)

---

## Session Length
- **Single dungeon:** ~1 hour
- **Full playthrough:** ~8-10 hours
- **100% completion:** ~15-20 hours (all endings, side quests)
